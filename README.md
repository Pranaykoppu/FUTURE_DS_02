# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard analyzes customer churn behavior, retention trends, and key business metrics to help businesses understand why customers leave and identify strategies to improve customer retention.

This project was completed as part of the **Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship (Task 2)**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Customer churn directly impacts business growth and revenue. The objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior, identify churn patterns, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn rate.
- Measure customer retention performance.
- Identify high-risk customer segments.
- Study customer tenure and monthly charges.
- Compare churn across contract types and internet services.
- Generate business insights and recommendations.

---

## 📂 Dataset

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Overall customer churn rate is **26.54%**.
- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** have the highest churn.
- **Fiber Optic** customers churn more frequently than DSL customers.
- Customers using **Electronic Check** are more likely to leave.
- Customers with shorter tenure are at a higher risk of churning.
- Long-term contract customers show significantly better retention.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch to yearly or two-year contracts by offering discounts.
- Improve customer onboarding during the first year.
- Enhance support quality for Fiber Optic customers.
- Offer personalized retention campaigns for Electronic Check users.
- Introduce loyalty rewards for long-term customers.
